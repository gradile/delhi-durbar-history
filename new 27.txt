1892 - 1934 H.H. Meherban Shrimant Maharaja Sir Malharrao Narayanrao Maharaj Pawar [Baba Sahib], Maharaja of Dewas (Junior), KCSI (1.1.1917), KIH gold medal (14.6.1912). b. 10th August 1877, elder son of Shrimant Jivajirao Haibatrao Maharaj Pawar [Khase Sahib], of Kothi Savasthan, sometime Heir Apparent of Dewas (Junior), educ. Daly Coll, Indore, and with Imperial Cadet Corps, Dehra Dun. Adopted by his paternal uncle, 18th January 1892 and succeeded him when he died the following day. Installed on the gadi, 22nd September 1892 and reigned under a Council of Regency until he came of age. Attended the Coronation Durbar at Delhi in 1903. Invested with full ruling powers, 10th August 1913. Granted the hereditary title of Maharaja, 1st January 1918. Rcvd: Delhi Durbar gold medals (1903 and 1911). m. (first) at Poona, 16th March 1895, H.H. Shrimant Akhand Soubhagyavati Maharani Mhalsa Bai Maharaje Sahib Pawar (b. ca. 1881; d.s.p. at Dewas, 8th November 1908), daughter of Meherban Shrimant Sardar Shahajirao Bhonsle, a first class sardar of Sawantwadi State. m. (second) (morganatic) at Dewas, 1912, Shrimant Akhand Soubhagyavati Prabhavati Bai Maharaje Sahib Pawar (b. at Goa, Portuguese India), née Indira Bai, daughter of Krishna Bai Salgaonkar, of Bombay, a Saraswati Brahmin originally from Goa in Portuguese India. He d. at Dewas, from heart failure, 4th February 1934 having had issue, one son and one daughter by his second wife:
1) Shrimant Maharaj Martandrao Malharrao [Dada Sahib] Bal Raje Puar. b. at Dewas, 19th February 1919 (excluded from the succession on account of the morganatic nature of his parents' marriage), educ. He was k. in a house fire at Colinton, Darjeeling, 1951.
1) Shrimant Maharajkumari Shashiprabha Raje Sahib Puar [Shrimant Akhand Soubhagyavati Shashiprabha Raje Sahib Walia]. b. at Dewas, 1st December 1921. m. Sardar Kamaljit Singh Wallia, from Indore. She had issue, one son and two daughters.

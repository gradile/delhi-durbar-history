The idea of group left and right appeared when I began to study the photos in more detail. Many elephants had different men walking on their sides so I made this distinction to identify each set.
By this time I played with some photos of the Main Procession for an ad file. They were not accurate because I only had 4 elephants with their men by then, but it helped with the possibility of visualizing the groups. I added Kashmir and Alwar to a couple of photos. They were not walking close in the procession, but I just placed them there just to get the idea. The perspective of the photos had to be the right one for them to not look strange.

---------------------------------------------------------

The following set was an elephant. My first. It was very difficult for me to make it without Daniel. So far I had to only worry about the painting, but now I had to model it! I chose an excellent photo, and made sure that I had more photos to view the different angles. It was the State Elephant of Jind.

I was never sure of objectively how it turned to be. I still feel not right about it, for strange reasons. Maybe that's why I never made the guards that go with the elephant. Maybe some day I will feel better about this group. The feeling washed with the different following new elephants. Each of them became more and more natural for me. Right now, Daniel made 11 elephants and I made 31. I learned to accept the numbers as they are with time.

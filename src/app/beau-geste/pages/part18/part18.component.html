Chapter 18

Following with the history of the Delhi Durbar 1903 Beau Geste sets, the next one was a new state elephant. This time it was Bundi's turn. The rajah was an amazing character, and his elephant was very beautiful. I could count on Menpes color plate, and many photos (luckily!). It was my second elephant, and I was more comfortable with the idea by then. I enjoyed making it very much.

Beau Geste Set 303 - State Elephant of Bundi, Delhi Durbar, 1903.

The following sets were mainly escort groups. Collectors loved to have their elephants surrounded by the men who were in the parade accompanying the state, forming a whole group. We made three bands for the elephants for which we had only made the guards (Alwar, Indore and Kashmir) and new guards, servants and musicians for the state of Gwalior.

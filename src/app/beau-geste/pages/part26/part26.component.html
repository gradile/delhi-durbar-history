Chapter 26

The Beau Geste sets that followed the Rewah cavalry and the Sehr & Mokalla flagbearers were 2 elephants. Both not belonging to the Main Procession itself but both being present in the second group of elephants that followed the two columns of the Procession on December 29, and obviously, both present at the Retainers Review.
I was regularly buying digital copies from the British Library by then. The Patiala retainers came up on a couple of photographs, and I decided to make them almost as soon as I got the image files. These are my digital copies.

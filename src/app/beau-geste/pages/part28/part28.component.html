Looking for more information about Datia, to make the group larger, I found some cavalrymen in one of the photographs that I bought on eBay. This photo showed one of Datia's elephants at the Retainers Review. It was an armoured elephant carrying a flag. As the definition of the photo was very neat, I found a beautiful group at the back. I found more information about them in other photos and text.

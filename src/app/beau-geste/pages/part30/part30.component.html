Chapter 30

The set to follow was a new elephant. The State Elephant of Rewa. It was April 2016. I had been postponing this elephant for some time. Making all those men standing around the howda was a great challenge for me. But I resolved the sculpting in the best and safest way. I always have to think about safety when shipping. I can make anything to remain in my studio, but facing the challenge of traveling long distances packed has always been something I need to think beforehand. I know many of you have this elephant, and I am very happy for that. It makes me proud that the Rewa Elephant is standing on shelves all over the world.
I was fortunate to find many photos of the elephant. Many times I am not able to find pictures taken from all sides.

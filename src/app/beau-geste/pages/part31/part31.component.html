Chapter 31

Here is more history of the Beau Geste sets of the Delhi Durbar 1903. Following the Rewa group came another spectacular elephant and its group: the State Elephant of Narsingharh and the walking group. I chose it because my last batch of digital copies acquired from the British Library included a photograph that was astounding. It showed the Narsingharh elephant walking alone, in all its splendor. Another photo bought through eBay showed the elephant walking at the Amphitheatre during the Retainers Review. Many guards and servants were pictured there, as well as the details of the howda. Both photographs were crucial in the making of the elephant. I wish I could always have such good resource information.

On May 2024, I've spent a few days in London researching at the British Library one more time. It was a bittersweet visit, since they still were very affected by the cyber-attack that they suffered in November, 2023. I was planning on making a full research about the photographers who were present at the Durbar, looking for possibilities of new photographs within their personal information. I had focused this time on that point of view. Instead, I found that they still have no system that could allow me to do that, even on site. I was hoping to sit on one of their computers and get a list of material, but there were no computers on this time, their system wasn't even available within their installations. Nor they were able to take any orders for digital copies. Not happy with that at all.
So this time I reviewed a couple of albums, with much more detail. I retook many pictures but mainly, it gave me a lot of time to really observe the photographs from a different perspective and think.
And there was something that stood up to me from this. The elephants' parade has always been the main focus of all the photographs but for the first time I noticed the surroundings. The number of spectators is astounding! I felt that I could never make enough of them to really reflect reality. So I decided to add more attendees sets, more variations of people watching so collectors can enhance their displays. I also decided to include backdrops for the displays, building fronts, monuments, trees.
I will release them between elephants. I still need to give it more planning, but the decision is made. 😊

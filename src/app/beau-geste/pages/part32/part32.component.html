At this point, I came to a turning point for Beau Geste, and our Delhi Durbar Line. I felt that I couldn't focus on as many subjects as we had been making so far. World War one, French Foreign Legion, Napoleonics and XVIII and XIX Centuries. Each subject deserved time and dedication, research and sculpting, that I couldn't give them any more. My personal research has always been the Delhi Durbar from 1903, and I was feeling that my interest should be directed only that way. So my decision was to make only Delhi Durbar sets from then and on. And to sell directly to collectors myself. Most of you know that I am always in touch with the Delhi Durbar followers, you have asked as many questions as you liked, made comments and added opinions and suggestions. I share with you all my research and my plans. That couldn't happen through retailers. And that was the kind of closeness I was aiming for. I knew that was what I wanted to follow my line, and that was the right path for me.
So the next release was the first group on the limited editions. I wanted to make only a few sets of each, to cover all my collectors and their needs, and then focus on a new idea and following release. The first elephant of the limited editions was the State Elephant of Rajgarh. I put all my effort in the sculpture. But my main incentive was that I was going to paint all the elephants myself. I had always been limited in the past, thinking on a kind of task that didn't go beyond my painters' skills. From now on, the only limit would be me. So I went for all the detail I always wanted. ALL. It was such a pleasure for me to be able to paint with the intricacy I've always wished. There was no limit any more. Any color, any pattern, any design.
The Rajgarh elephant is an example of this. It is one of my favorites, specially being the first one that I design to make myself from the beginning to the end. It is as colorful and detailed as I planned it to be, and I LOVED the result. It was such a joy to make, and still is a pleasure for me to make it.

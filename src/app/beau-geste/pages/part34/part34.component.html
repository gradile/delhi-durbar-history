Chapter 34

This chapter features a few sets from the same state, Charkkhari, which I think is one of the most colorfull groups I made. I feel that the pictures don't do them justice at all.
One of the digital copies that I had from the British Library showed one of the elephants is such neat detail, which such focus that the decision to make the Charkkhari elephant was an easy one. Added to that, I had another digital copy where a delightful group of camel riders from the same state was shown during the Retainers Review. The group became one of the most successful within the Beau Geste Delhi Durbar line.

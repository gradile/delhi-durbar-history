And then the following elephant. One of my favorites for many reasons. The maharaja was superb. He sat alone on the howda's front. He was richly dressed and his jewelry was incredible. The reports said that the emerald hanging on his forehead was the size of a chicken egg. I also really like how the elephant's clothes turned out. The colors, the patterns. I definitely love it. Glad that so many of you already decided to include it in your collections.
The State Elephant of Bahawalpur walked on the left column of the Main Procession, beside the Sikkim elephant, behind Patiala and Sehr and Mokalla, and in front of Nabha and Cooch Behar.

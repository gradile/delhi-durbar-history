The following group of sets was the Sehr & Mokalla state. I released them in September 2019 and brought the sets to the Chicago show that year. It is a very colorful group, very unique and vivacious. The State Elephant of Sehr & Mokalla walked on the right column beside the Elephant of Patiala, both being 15° within the Main Procession.

The Makala group had always called my attention. I had a digital copy bought from the British Library for a group of their people, and with time I got more photos of the group, mainly thanks to my friend Bill. Flagbearers, Spearbearers, Standarbearers and Camel Riders. I had already made the soldiers, musicians and flagbearers, so I decided to complete the group with the two huge flagbearers, the unique spearers and the camelry.

Continuing with the history of the Beau Geste Delhi Durbar sets, the following group was one of the most special. The elephant is one of the most colorful, the maharaja had a lot of character, and the umbrellas, signs and spears' group are very unique.
State Elephant of Maler Kotla walked alone, close to the end of the Main Procession, 22nd in the middle of both columns. In front of the elephant walked Tehri and Bansda, and behind it were Limri and Faridkot. It was one of the first elephants I had in photos from a close spot (Bell's) even when I didn't know to which state it belonged to. In my opinion, is one of the most beautiful elephants that I have made. And it is one of our best sellers. It was released in September 2020, and collectors keep on choosing it still, more than 4 years later.

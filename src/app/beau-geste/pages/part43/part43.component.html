Chapter 43

By September 2021, our new elephant with group was the State Elephant of Rampur. The state came to existance in 1774. After independence in 1947 it merged with others in the area and conformed the United Provinces. The elephant walked 19º on the right column of the Main Procession, behind Hill Tippera and Jind, beside Kapurthala and in front of Benares and Sirmur. The Nawab's name was Sir Sayyid Ali Khan Bahadur of Rampur.

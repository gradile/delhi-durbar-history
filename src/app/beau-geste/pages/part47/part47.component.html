At this point, I'm completely sure that this 'Kolhapur' elephant is in fact Narsingharh's. That means that the 'Narsingharh' Elephant that I made in September 2016 is actually Kolhapur's. As I made them both, it's no big deal really. I just wanted you to be aware of that. As I don't have any official proof of that, this is just off the record

Following the Delhi Main Procession Route, we got to the point where the elephants arrived at the end of Chandni Chawk and turned right, marching to take Queen's Road one more time. There is a mosque at this point, the Footehpoorie Masjid, from where most of the photographs were taken for this spot. Lots of people congregated in this corner, as you can see in the pictures from 1903. Nowadays, it's even more crowded

The red star shows the Clock Tower in the far distance, and the festive line of flags that decorated across the street, in front of the Town Hall

In this case, the red star is the Fatehpoori Masjid today

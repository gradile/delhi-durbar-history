Mi name is Ana Donzino, and I am the owner, manager and artist of Beau Geste (New Toy Soldiers), from Argentina.

I came across the Delhi Durbar 1903 in 2000, and never let it go. I've been researching about the subject since then, and the result has been a large line of miniatures that represent the period.

Our website is www.beau-geste.com and you can contact me at info@beau-geste.com. You can subscribe to my newsletters and browse all the lines that we make. Enjoy!
